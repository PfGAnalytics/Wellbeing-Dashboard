A comprehensive framework called SEEDs (Social, Economic, and Environmental Domains) has been developed to assess the Programme for Government and shape its future versions. It focuses on three crucial areas—Social, Economic, and Environmental—that contribute to overall wellbeing.
We have developed a set of Indicators composed of Official and National Statistics. By analysing the evidence provided by these Indicators, we can gain valuable insights into Northern Ireland’s wellbeing, the areas where we are making progress, and where progress still needs to be made.
